# Sprint 2 Test Guide

## New weapons
- Spread Shot: violet five-way fire.
- Railgun: orange heavy shot with piercing.
- Homing Missiles: gold tracking projectiles.
- Overdrive: temporary rapid fire.
- Damage Boost: temporary x2 damage.

## New power-ups
- EMP: freezes enemies temporarily.
- Repair: restores one hull point.
- New weapon power-ups enter the normal drop table.

## New enemies
- Sniper: stops near the top, telegraphs, then fires a fast shot.
- Kamikaze: locks onto the player and charges.
- Shield Carrier: absorbs multiple hits before taking hull damage.
- Medic: heals nearby damaged enemies.
- Heavy Cruiser: high health and triple fire.

## New stage
- Stage 4: ION STORM FRONTIER showcases the new enemy roster.
- Stage 5+ continues with the infinite generator using all unlocked enemy types.
